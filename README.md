# SplitSense 💸  
### Smart Expense Splitting with Social Insights  

SplitSense is a modern web application that helps groups of friends, roommates, or teams manage shared expenses in a fun, engaging, and intelligent way.  

Unlike traditional bill-splitting apps, SplitSense not only tracks expenses but also analyzes repayment habits and generates **Debt Personalities**, leaderboards, streaks, and group insights.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Managing shared expenses in friend groups, college trips, flats, and events often leads to:

- Confusion over who owes whom  
- Delayed repayments  
- Repeated reminders  
- Lack of transparency  
- Awkward money conversations  

SplitSense solves this by making expense management simple, transparent, and socially engaging.

---

## 💡 Unique Selling Point

SplitSense transforms a boring utility into an interactive experience by introducing:

- **Debt Personality Profiles**  
- **Repayment Streaks**  
- **Leaderboards**  
- **Group Insights Dashboard**  
- **Fun Reminders & Gamification**

---

## ✨ Features

### 👥 Group Management
- Create groups (Trip, Flatmates, Friends, Office Team)
- Add members
- Manage group expenses

### 💰 Expense Tracking
- Add shared expenses
- Select who paid
- Split equally / custom split
- Auto-calculate balances

### 📊 Dashboard & Analytics
- Total group spending
- Individual contributions
- Pending repayments
- Spending charts

### 🧠 Debt Personality Engine
Each user gets a fun repayment personality based on behavior:

Examples:
- Instant Settler ⚡  
- Chronic Delayer 🐢  
- Calculator King 🧮  
- Generous Sponsor 🎉  
- Last Minute Payer ⏰  

### 🏆 Gamification
- Fastest payer leaderboard
- Longest repayment streak
- Most generous member

### 🔔 Reminders
- Pending payment alerts
- Friendly nudges
